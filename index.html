Sattva Parel Mumbai Overview

Sattva Parel represents the zenith of architectural excellence, redefining the iconic South Mumbai skyline. Spanning an expansive 2.25-acre land parcel, this 63-storey landmark by the esteemed Sattva Group is designed for those who demand a life of stature and tranquility. Offering a curated selection of premium 2 and 3 BHK luxury apartments, the project serves as an ultra-premium residential sanctuary in the heart of Parel. Strategically positioned to offer more than just a home, Sattva Parel provides residents with a rare vantage point featuring unobstructed, panoramic views of the Atal Setu Sea Link and the shimmering Mumbai coastline. To ensure an atmosphere of absolute privacy and peace, all habitable residences begin from the 13th floor, effectively elevating your lifestyle above the citys vibrant pulse. Each residence is a masterclass in spatial design, featuring expansive private sundecks and floor-to-ceiling windows that invite abundant natural light and cross-ventilation. This prime location combined with world-class craftsmanship makes Sattva Parel the definitive choice for sophisticated urban living in South Mumbai. Experience a lifestyle where every detail is meticulously crafted to reflect prestige, comfort, and modern elegance.
